The 21st Century Integrated Digital Experience Act (IDEA) was signed into law on December 20, 2018. The bill is designed to transform the federal government's digital service delivery through website modernization, improved accessibility, adoption of digital forms and signatures, and ultimately prioritize and accelerate the citizen and employee experience.

  FEATURED PRODUCT

Seagate FIPS 140-2 Compliant Self-Encrypting Drive Storage

Devices Now Available Through Carahsoft

Seagate’s drives are the first self-encrypting drives in the industry to achieve FIPS 140-2 Level 2 certification from National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) and the Canadian Communications Security Establishment (CSEC). FIPS (Federal Information Processing Standard) 140-2 is a U.S. government standard that describes the encryption and related security requirements IT products must meet for sensitive but unclassified (SBU) use. Seagate's FIPS 140-2 SEDS are available through Carahsoft Technology Corp.
